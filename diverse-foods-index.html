Re: Vegetarian/vegan options availability at St. Louis University (SLU)

Dear Richard,

A more diverse food choice selection

The polls are in and the results are evident. Students are requesting a wider variety of menu items, namely vegan/vegetarian options.

The demand for such a diverse menu is apparent. An overwhelming 8 in 10 students prefer vegan menu choices in the school cafeteria.

Our polls have comprised the most popular vegan items requested by students in ranked order:

  1. Dairy free cheese pizza
  2. Vegan chicken pad Thai
  3. Veggie pattie hamburgers
  4. Vegan General Tso's chicken
  5. Dairy-free frozen yogurt

Please consider the above changes to the menu options for the overwhelming majority of vegan students as indicated in the polls.

Lactose free menu items

In addition to vegan items, a whopping 7 in 10 students voted 'yes' to add lactose intolerant friendly foods to the cafeteria menu to include:

  • lactose friendly milk
  • lactose friendly yogurt
  • lactose friendly tiramisu

The results are in, the students would like a new and revamped cafeteria menu item selection. Please consider the above items when ordering food for the school.
